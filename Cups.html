19. LEAGUES TITLES

Liverpool have won a phenomenal 19 top-flight league titles, with Jürgen Klopp leading the Reds to their most recent in the Premier League in 2019-20.

6. EUROPEAN CUPS

The first came under Bob Paisley,who added 2 more to the trophy cabinet before his successor Joe Fagan also won the famous cup. Rafael Benitez oversaw the Miracle of Istanbul in 2005, and the most recent came under Jürgen Klopp in 2019.

1. FIFA CLUB WORLD CUP

Liverpool broke new ground in December 2019 by lifting the Club World Cup for the first time in Reds history.

2. WOMEN'S SUPER LEAGUE TITLES

Liverpool FC Women, know as LFC Ladies won their first WSL title in 2013 and would go on to repeat the feat in 2014 after a dramatic season finale saw them leapfrog from third to first.

7. FA CUP

In 1965, the club won its first FA Cup. In 1966, the club won the First Division but lost to Borussia Dortmund in the European Cup Winners' Cup final. Liverpool won both the League and the UEFA Cup during the 1972–73 season, and the FA Cup again a year laterLiverpool have seven FA Cups to their name. The first triumph came under Bill Shankly in 1965 when Leeds United were defeated 2-1 at Wembley..

9. LEAGUE CUPS

Liverpool won 4 consecutive titles between 1981-1984, a record equalled by Manchester City in 2021.Liverpool boast a record tally of 9 League Cups.The trophy was last brought home to Anfield in 2022 courtesy of a penalty shootout victory over Chelsea at Wembley.
